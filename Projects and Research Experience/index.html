Anyon Condensation in 3D Topological Orders

July 2022 - Present
Supervised by Yidun Wan

  • Studied topological orders and the language of unitary modular tensor categories.

Leakage in Topological Quantum Computation by Fibonacci Anyons

September 2021 - June 2022
Supervised by Yidun Wan

  • Studied the scheme of universal topological quantum computations, especially the case of Fibonacci anyons.
  • Searched for leakage-free entangling 2-qubit gates up to length 20 by Julia and verified that no such gate exists.

Simulating 2D Edwards-Anderson Spin Glass Models by Tensor Networks

June 2022
Final Project of Statistical Mechanics I

  • Studied the Edwards-Anderson spin glass model and tensor network renormalization (TNR).
  • Implemented TNR to simulate binomial and Gaussian Edwards-Anderson models with size up to $64\times 64$ by Python.

Accelerating the Solution of Optical Inverse Problems by GPUs

December 2021
Final Project of Optics

  • Studied optical inverse problems and optimization algorithms.
  • Implemented the gradient descent algorithm on GPUs to solve optical inverse problems by Python and CUDA.
